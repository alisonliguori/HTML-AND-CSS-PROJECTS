About Me

This is my draft website! Still a work in progress

Hi! My name is Alison Liguori and I love wildlife! Photography is my passion. I began wildlife photography in 2025 and instantly fell in love with it. I love all animals and dream of traveling the world to photograph them in their natural habitat. Not only does it bring me joy, but I hope my photos inspire others to care more about wildlife conservation. Humans are the most significant agent of ecological change! Nature has been around before us, yet we behave as if it revolves around us It is time to make a change!

Camera:

I use a Nikon D7500 and my primary lens for wildlife is a Tameron G2 600mm! I am saving up for a Nikon Z8!

I hope to buy it for my birthday!

"Look closely at nature. Every species is a masterpiece, exquisitely adapted to the particular environment in which it has survived. Who are we to destroy or even diminish biodiversity?
- E.O. Wilson"
